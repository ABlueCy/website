The following `vtctl` commands are available for administering workflows.

## Commands

### WorkflowCreate
 `WorkflowCreate  [-skip_start] <factoryName> [parameters...]`

### WorkflowStart
 `WorkflowStart  <uuid>`

### WorkflowStop
 `WorkflowStop  <uuid>`

### WorkflowDelete
 `WorkflowDelete  <uuid>`

### WorkflowWait
 `WorkflowWait  <uuid>`

### WorkflowTree
 `WorkflowTree  `

### WorkflowAction
 `WorkflowAction  <path> <name>`
